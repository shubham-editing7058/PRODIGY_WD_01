About Bali

Bali is an Indonesian island known for its forested volcanic mountains, iconic rice paddies, beaches and coral reefs. It's a popular destination for relaxation, culture, and adventure.

Top Attractions

Uluwatu Temple

Cliffside temple with sunset views and traditional dances.

Kuta Beach

Famous surfing beach with lively nightlife and shops.

Tegallalang Rice Terraces

Beautiful green terraces that showcase traditional Balinese irrigation.
